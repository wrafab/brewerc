Final Project

    • My final project idea was really cool and it super worked. The genreal idea was that I wanted to make my own lamp with led lights. The first stage was to cut up some bright color piping. The second stage was create a flat base. I used a square peice of wood, and round the corners so that it looked nicer. Then I had to create holes in the wood that were the right size as the piping. The right size as in big enough to fit and small enough to make sure the poles wouldn't slip out. The third stage was to appeal to the aesthic side of the project. I did multple layers of spray spaint so that each would show through and create a really nice color. The fourth stage was to raster some words or designs on my pipes. I went with the names of my two brothers, 'Javier' and "Elisha', and I also engraved the word family. The fifth stage which was the hardest was to saudert the LED lights for my lamp. On the LED strip of lights I had to smake sure i saudert the correct parts so that I could get the correct color, which is blue. After sauderting my LED lights I had to go back to my wood base and create channels on the bottom connecting the holes so that the wiring would go through and the base could lay flat. And then finally I wrapped a peice of cloth around the top to give it a nice hippie vibe.
